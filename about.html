Light Duty

Light Duty Towing Services

Santa Fe Towing proudly offers professional light-duty towing services across the entire state of New Mexico, safely transporting cars, SUVs, pickup trucks, motorcycles, and small vans whenever and wherever they’re needed. Equipped with modern flatbed tow trucks and wheel-lift systems, our trained operators handle everything from local moves within Santa Fe to long-distance tows across the state—whether it’s after a breakdown on I-25, a crash on I-40, or simply relocating a vehicle to a repair shop or new home. Heavy Duty Towing Services

With a powerful fleet of heavy-duty rotators, lowboy trailers, landoll trailers, and air-cushion recovery units, our certified operators can safely recover and transport even the largest vehicles after rollovers, off-road incidents, or mechanical failures on interstates or remote routes. Oversize Loads

Large Oversize Transportation

Santa Fe Towing offers professional, fully permitted oversize and overweight load transportation services across every corner of New Mexico & beyond, ensuring safe and compliant delivery of the state’s biggest cargo. From bulldozers, cranes, and transformers to wind blades, modular homes, and drilling rigs, our specialized fleet—including steerable multi-axle lowboys, RGNs, perimeter-frame trailers, and heavy-haul tractors—handles loads that exceed standard legal limits in length, width, height, or weight. Our in-house permitting team secures New Mexico DOT and local permits quickly, while certified pilot car drivers, route surveys, and police escorts guarantee smooth passage on I-40, I-25, US-54, or remote back roads. Operating 24/7 with night and weekend availability, Santa Fe Towing moves critical equipment to oilfields near Carlsbad, wind farms near La Joya, construction projects in Santa Fe, and mines near Silver City—always on schedule, fully insured, and with an unmatched safety record that keeps New Mexico’s industries moving.
